Rockbot

A bot where you collect rocks NFT-style: marketplace, buying, selling and trading. In beta and under full renovation.

What is it?

Rockbot is a bot where you collect rocks in pure NFT style: each rock is a piece to collect, trade and sell. It includes a marketplace, buying/selling and trading between users, plus all the features of a general-purpose bot.

History

It used to be called utybot. After retiring it, I picked it back up and renamed it to Rockbot, giving it a top-to-bottom renovation.

Status

Currently in beta and under renovation, so some things may change while I polish it.
