## What is it?

**espc.lat** is this site: my **personal wiki**. The idea is to have a single place
—with an encyclopedia look— to tell who I am and, above all, to document every project
I build, each with its own page and info.

## How it's built

- **Astro** as a static site generator (fast and lightweight).
- Content in **Markdown**: each project is a file, so growing it is trivial.
- A **Wikipedia-like** style (articles, infobox, blue links) combined with a
  **bento grid** on the home page.
- **Bilingual** (Spanish / English) and with **light/dark mode**.
- Deployed on **GitHub Pages** with a custom domain.
