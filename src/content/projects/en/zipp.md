## What was it?

**Zipp.so** started as a **smart link shortener**. It was a draft of mine that even
managed to generate some **income**.

## Why it was deprecated

Because of the **high expectations** around it, I chose to let it go: I knew that a
project like this, done well and at scale, needed a **solid budget** to sustain its
costs. So it became a closed chapter.

> 🪦 **Deprecated** project. I keep it documented as part of the journey.
